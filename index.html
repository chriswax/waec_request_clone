INTRODUCTION

This is an online self-service portal aimed at allowing for our esteemed stakeholders and the public to conveniently access services offered by the Council in Nigeria.

HOW IT WORKS
  • Check for your desired service
  • Click on purchase token under the desired service
  • Select the desired service and fill other boxes on the page
  • Proceed to online payment. After payment, you will receive two notifications via email showing your receipt and your token
  • Once you click on purchase token, the transaction reference no is displayed. It is also displayed on the receipt after payment.
  • Click on make request on the home page and input your token, other required details as well as upload the required documents. Please note that the uploaded documents should be uploaded as a single pdf document
  • Click on submit and your request is sent to an officer for processing
  • If there is need for more information or clarification on your request, you will be contacted via the portal
ALREADY HAVE A TOKEN?

If you have bought a token, you have completed the first step. Next, kindly click on Make Request button to select your request type and complete the process. You can always track the status of your request by clicking on the Track Request button. If you did not receive your token, click on Re-query Transaction, type in your reference number and your token will be displayed.

Do you know there are two types of request tokens?
1
REQUEST FOR INFORMATION

This only gives you the necessary information and requirements that must be met before you apply for service request. For instance, if you buy token to request information about Attestation of Result, the token will only give you information on requirements and necessary documents you need to provide to access this service. The token does not give you access to make request for the service itself.

2
REQUEST FOR SERVICE

This token gives you access to apply for a service request.
It is expedient you already have all necessary requirements needed to apply for the service request.
